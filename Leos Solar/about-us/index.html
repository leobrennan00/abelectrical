Journey

Our Milestones and Achievements Over Time

Since our founding, Leo's Solar has been dedicated to providing sustainable energy solutions. Explore our journey through key milestones that highlight our commitment to innovation and excellence.

2015

Company Founded

Leo's Solar was established with a mission to transform the energy landscape. Our goal was to make solar energy accessible to everyone.

2017

First Major Project

We successfully completed our first large-scale solar installation, setting a benchmark in the industry. This project marked a significant step in our growth and reputation.

2020

Expansion of Services

We expanded our offerings to include energy storage solutions and smart home integrations. This diversification allowed us to better serve our customers' evolving needs.

2023

Sustainability Commitment

We launched initiatives to promote sustainability and community engagement. Our focus remains on reducing carbon footprints and enhancing energy efficiency.
